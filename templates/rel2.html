Judaism

Bar/Bat Mitzvah

Bar Mitzvah is a Jewish ritual that signifies a coming of age for boys, while Bat Mitzvah signifies a coming of age for girls. Both celebrate the progression of maturity of a boy or a girl in regards to his/her Jewish identity as well as his/her connection to the Torah and Mitzvot.

The ritual of Bar/Bat Mitzvah also is known for bringing together Jewish communities. These celebrations oftentimes bring together large groups of people that include family, friends, and religious acquaintances. They help to create an environment in which Jewish people can come together to celebrate the coming of age for a loved one.
